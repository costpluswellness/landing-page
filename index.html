Cost Plus Wellness

An Open-Source Health Care Initiative from Mark Cuban

April 4, 2025

Misaligned incentives have broken our healthcare system. Self-funded employers - covering 100 million Americans - are locked into opaque contracts, with no visibility into negotiated rates and no way to meaningfully impact employee health outcomes.

Meanwhile, providers are stuck chasing down patients for collection, often forced to raise prices just to manage the financial risk of unpaid deductibles set by health insurance companies. Both the providers and employers (the purchasers) are handcuffed by denials that aren‚Äôt clinically relevant and utilization management reviews that only serve to delay care. As a result, costs increase and employee productivity plummets.

We created Cost Plus Wellness to reimagine health care from the ground up.

Cost Plus Wellness is an open-source project that empowers employers to work directly with health care providers in a transparent way. Our model is simple: employers contract directly with providers and guarantee full and prompt payment for services rendered. That means no out-of-pocket costs for the employee, no prior authorizations, and no delays in collecting payments for the provider.
